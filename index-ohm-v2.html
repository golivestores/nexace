The Nexace story
We started Nexace because the reformers we loved belonged only to studios — heavy, industrial, out of reach. So we rebuilt the machine from the frame up: studio-grade strength, the silence of a well-made instrument, and lines refined enough to belong in any room of your home — because a daily practice shouldn't have to wait for a class on the calendar.
