Studio-grade reformer pilates equipment engineered for home practice. Handcrafted solid wood frame, whisper-quiet rail system, and 5-level color-coded spring resistance for every skill level.

The NX901 Classic is Nexace's flagship reformer, built with traditional mortise-and-tenon joinery and premium solid beech wood. Designed to deliver a studio-quality workout in the comfort of your home, it features an ultra-smooth aluminum rail system and high-density cushioning for comfort during extended sessions.
