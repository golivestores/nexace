Our Story · Since 2012

The Craft

01
Design

Every reformer begins as a sketch in our Melbourne studio. Instructors, engineers and physio consultants refine each curve, joint and pressure point before a single piece is cut.

02
Craft

Frames are laminated from quarter-sawn maple and brushed aircraft-grade aluminum. Rails are honed to glide in silence. Each spring is hand-tensioned, color-coded and logged to a serial number.

03
Test

Before any reformer leaves our workshop, it passes a studio-grade checklist — spring calibration, rail glide, headrest pitch, strap tension — inspected and signed by the lead craftsman.

Melbourne Workshop · Australia Our team hand-finishes every Nexace reformer — nine artisans, one bench, twelve years of practice.

Melbourne · Australia

Built where we train.

Nexace is built out of a single workshop outside Melbourne by a team of nine artisans — cabinet-makers, upholsterers, and reformer instructors who've spent decades on the equipment they now build. Every frame, spring, rail and strap passes through our own hands. The first NX901 took nine months and thirty-four revisions. Twelve years on, we still build them the same way: slowly, carefully, one reformer at a time.

Why Nexace

Studio-grade quality. Built for the home.

Studio-Grade
01 · Verified Quality

Every spring calibrated. Every rail tested.

Each reformer is inspected and signed off against a 34-point studio checklist — spring tension logged to the newton, rail glide measured in milliseconds, strap length cross-verified. A calibration certificate ships with every unit.

Whisper-Quiet
02 · Engineered Silence

Quiet enough for a sleeping household.

Felt-lined carriage runners, machined aluminum rails and dampened springs mean no clunks, no whirs, no announcement to the rest of the house. Train at dawn, train at midnight — nobody else has to know.

Lifetime-Backed
03 · Built to Outlast

Five years on the frame. Lifetime on the springs.

We guarantee the frame for five years and the springs forever. One email replaces any worn part at cost — because a reformer should outlast the room it sits in, not the trend that bought it.

Risk-Free · 30 Days
04 · Home Trial

Thirty days in your living room. On us.

Train on your reformer for a full month. If it's not the right fit, we pick it up — no restocking fee, no return shipping, no questions. Because the only way to know a reformer is yours is to live with it.
